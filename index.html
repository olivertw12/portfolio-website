Capstone Project

DMARC Network Analysis

Leveraging R and geospatial analysis to uncover service gaps in the DMARC Food Pantry Network. By merging transaction logs with ACS Census data, I mapped visitor density to identify high-need areas and utilized predictive modeling to forecast a 2025 surge in demand within specific demographic groups.
