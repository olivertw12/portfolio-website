Brand Identity

Pyrkia Health

Client Pyrkia Health Services
Role Logo Design
Year 2024
Deliverables Logo, Brand Assets
01

Background

Pyrkia Health Services is a nature-focused health program dedicated to holistic wellness. Their mission is to integrate physical health with the restorative power of the natural world, fostering a sense of unity between the individual and their environment. They commissioned a new visual identity to better communicate these core values to potential clients.

02

The Challenge

The health and wellness sector is crowded with generic imagery. The client needed a logo that was distinct yet immediately recognizable as a symbol of care and unity. The challenge was to create a mark that felt organic and human-centered without relying on clichéd health tropes, effectively capturing the client's specific vision of "holistic wellness."

03

The Solution

I developed the client's vision by focusing on an abstract figure that symbolizes the human element within the natural world. To reinforce the concepts of completeness and self-emphasis, I integrated this figure with a prominent abstract circle. This geometric form represents the cycle of health, unity, and the holistic nature of the program.

04

The Result

The final design is a clean, modern logo that successfully bridges the gap between professional healthcare and organic wellness. The abstract circle and figure create a memorable icon that anchors the brand's visual identity, providing a strong foundation for future marketing materials, merchandise, and digital presence.
