Mainframe Studios

Client Mainframe Studios
Role Visual Design
Year 2023
Deliverables Poster, Typeface
01

Background

Mainframe Studios is one of the largest non-profit art studios in the nation, providing permanent, affordable workspaces for hundreds of artists in Des Moines, Iowa. They are known for their vibrant creative community and their visually iconic building, which features a massive, distinctively painted exterior. As a central hub for the arts, they required a strong visual identity that could capture this unique, creative energy for their promotional materials.

02

The Challenge

The studio needed a unique poster design that was not only promotional but also served as an authentic representation of its brand. The primary challenge was to capture the essence of their most recognizable asset—the colorful, geometric-painted exterior—without simply resorting to photography. The task was to translate the large-scale architectural art into a cohesive, functional, and original visual language that could be deployed on a printed poster.

03

The Solution

To solve this, the design process began with an in-depth analysis of the studio's exterior, deconstructing its geometric patterns and bold color palette. These core shapes and angles were then abstracted and systematized to build the letterforms of a new, bespoke display typeface. This custom font, directly inspired by and derived from the building's architecture, became the central anchor of the poster's design. The final composition heavily featured this unique typeface, using a color palette sampled directly from the building's facade.

04

The Result

The resulting poster provided Mainframe Studios with a powerful and original piece of visual communication that was a true extension of its physical identity. The design was highly successful and received significant external recognition. The poster was juried and selected to be displayed in the Anderson Gallery for their 52nd Annual Juried Student Exhibition, where it ultimately won a prestigious award for graphic design.
