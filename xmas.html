Christmas in Excelsior

Client International Lions Club
Role Map Illustration
Year 2024
Deliverables Event Map, Pamphlet
01

Background

The International Lions Club organized the first-ever Christmas Market in Excelsior, a major community initiative aimed at bringing residents and visitors together for the holiday season. As a volunteer-led effort, the event was ambitious in scope, anticipating an estimated attendance of 50,000 people over the course of the market.

02

The Challenge

With such a significant influx of visitors to a new event layout, clarity and navigation were critical safety and logistical concerns. Attendees needed to be able to intuitively find specific vendor zones, food areas, restrooms, and event features. Without a clear guide, the risk of congestion and confusion for the 50,000 guests was high.

03

The Solution

I volunteered to design a comprehensive area map that prioritized readability and functional navigation. The design clearly highlighted efficient walking routes, distinct vendor zones, and key landmarks. The layout was optimized for print, ensuring it was legible even when scaled down for the event pamphlet.

04

The Result

The map became a central piece of the visitor experience, distributed in printed pamphlets to the estimated audience of 50,000. It successfully directed foot traffic, reduced bottlenecks, and helped families navigate the market features with ease, contributing to the overall success of Excelsior's inaugural Christmas Market.
